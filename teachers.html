Honest answers

You might be thinking...

We would rather address your concerns head-on than let them linger.

"What if the assessment places my student lower than I suggested?"

This is the most important question — and we are glad you are asking it. The report never evaluates your teaching. It evaluates the student's current spoken performance under real conditions. If the result is lower than expected, the report frames this as a starting point, not a judgement. And here is the thing teachers tell us: when a student sees their real level in black and white, their motivation to work harder with you goes up, not down. The report literally recommends continued work with their current teacher as the path forward.

"Will my students think I am just trying to earn a commission?"

Your students do not see your commission. They do not know the percentage. They do not know the structure. What they see is a professional assessment tool that their teacher recommended — the same way a doctor recommends a blood test. Nobody suspects the doctor of having a referral deal with the lab. You are recommending a diagnostic because it makes your teaching better. The commission is simply how we thank you for that recommendation.

"Is this just another affiliate programme that pays pennies?"

30% is not an affiliate rate — it is a partnership rate. On a Full Assessment at €129, you earn €38.70 per student. Refer eight students a month and that is over €300 of recurring monthly income from something you would recommend anyway. There are no tiers to unlock, no minimum thresholds, no expiry dates. The rate is 30%, always. We would rather have fewer partners who genuinely believe in the product than an army of affiliates pasting links.

"What if my students do not want to pay for an assessment?"

This is where your professional recommendation changes the equation entirely. When a student googles "language assessment" they see a price and hesitate. When their trusted teacher says "I want you to take this assessment so I can build your next three months of lessons around real data" — that is not a purchase, that is a prescription. The students who book through teacher referrals have the highest completion rate and the highest satisfaction scores. Your recommendation is what transforms a service into a necessity.

"How do I know LingoGrade assessments are actually good?"

Every assessment is conducted by a certified language professional using the full CEFR framework — the same standard behind Goethe-Institut, telc, and Cambridge examinations. The report includes detailed error analysis across grammar, vocabulary, fluency, coherence, and pronunciation — with specific examples from the student's own speech. This is not a chatbot quiz. This is a structured, human-led diagnostic that produces a report you can build an entire teaching plan around.

"What does it cost me to join?"

Nothing. There is no signup fee, no monthly charge, no minimum commitment. You sign up, you get your link, you start earning. If you refer zero students, it costs you nothing. If you refer fifty, you earn 30% on all fifty. The risk is entirely ours.
