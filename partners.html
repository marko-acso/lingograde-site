Requirements

What we look for in a partner

We maintain high standards so every learner receives a world-class assessment experience.

  • C2 proficiency — Native or certified C2 level in at least one of our 12 supported languages
  • Teaching or assessment experience — Demonstrated experience in language teaching, tutoring, or formal assessment
  • Reliable internet connection — Stable broadband capable of high-quality video and audio calls
  • Shure MV7+ microphone — Required for all assessments (provided during onboarding)
  • Quiet, professional space — A dedicated, distraction-free environment for conducting assessments

Honest Answers

You probably have concerns. Let’s address them.

“You’re probably wondering if this is worth your time.”

Fair question. If you refer just 5 students a month, you’re earning €270/month for work you already do — recommending assessments to people who need them. No cold calling, no selling. Just sharing a link.

“You might think this sounds too good to be true.”

We understand. The revenue share is real because our margins allow it — the assessment is delivered by AI-assisted analysis, not manual labor. Your referral is the hardest part to scale. That’s why we pay for it.

“What if my students don’t like the assessment?”

Every assessment is conducted by Marko personally. 25 minutes of genuine conversation, not a test. Students consistently say it was the most honest feedback they’ve ever received. If they’re not satisfied, the revenue share still stands — we take the risk, not you.

“Is there a minimum commitment?”

No. No minimum referrals, no monthly quotas, no contracts to break. Refer one student or fifty. Your link stays active. Your revenue share stays the same.

“What if I just want to try it first?”

Good instinct. Apply, get your link, refer one student. See what happens. If it’s not for you, walk away. No exit fee, no hard feelings. But most partners don’t leave — because the first payout changes everything.
