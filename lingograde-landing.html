What You Get

A complete picture of your language skills

Every assessment is structured into clear blocks. The base package includes Block A and B. Add-ons unlock Blocks C, D, and E.

Block A

CEFR Assessment

Active and passive CEFR scores with confidence rating. Structural stability analysis. Error class identification. This is your diagnostic baseline.

Included in all packages
Block B

Errors & Solutions

Your key structural errors explained in plain language — not just what's wrong, but why it happens and exactly how to fix it. With concrete examples from your conversation.

Included in all packages
Block C

Personalized Homework

Three types of exercises targeting your specific weak points: structural writing, precision drills, and guided speaking practice. Ready to start immediately.

Block E

8-Week Learning Plan

A structured week-by-week plan designed to take you to the next CEFR level. Each phase builds on the previous one with clear milestones.

Block D

Native Language Translation

Your complete student report translated into your mother tongue. Perfect for beginners who need to fully understand every recommendation.
