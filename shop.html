What counts

  • Sticker on a fixed, permanent surface — laptop, wall, window, lamp post, bus stop, campus board
  • Your face — clearly visible, no guessing
  • Marco kissed — plush held close, lips on Marco, his face visible
  • Sticker in frame — sharp, readable, within 30° angle
  • Unlimited locations — every new spot earns commission via QR + EUR 5 selfie bonus

Good to know before you snap

  • Place stickers on a fixed, permanent surface rather than handheld or loose items
  • Make sure your face is clearly visible — no sunglasses or masks
  • Include Marco in the shot with a kiss pose, sticker sharp and in frame
  • Use the original photo — no digital edits, filters, or overlays
  • Each location counts once — find a new spot for the next one
  • Dress appropriately for the setting (swimwear at beach/pool is fine)
