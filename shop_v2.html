Sticker Guide

Place it. Snap it. Earn it.

Right now, EUR 5 is waiting for you per sticker. The only question is how many you will claim before someone else does.

Step 1

Stick it right

Every sticker is a silent salesperson working for you 24/7. Every surface without one is a conversation that never starts — a referral you will never earn back.

Your world: laptop lid, desk, notebook cover. These are the surfaces people already see every day — claim them first.

The street: lamp post, bus stop, park bench, shop window. Hundreds of eyes, zero effort. Your sticker does the talking while you sleep.

The crowd: café, gym, co-working space, library, university campus board. High-traffic, high-curiosity environments where people actually scan things.

Fixed, permanent surfaces only. Plant your flags where they stay.

Step 2

Kiss Marco

This is your proof of commitment — the moment that turns a sticker into earnings. It seems like you have already done the hard part. All that is left is the photo.

Three things in frame: your face, Marco's face, and the sticker on the surface. Kiss the plush. One snap. Done.

Step 3

Earn EUR 5

Upload. Within 24 hours, EUR 5 lands in your account. That is it. The only way to miss it is to not upload. By uploading you agree to publishing on our website.

What counts

  • Sticker on a fixed, permanent surface — laptop, wall, window, lamp post, bus stop, campus board
  • Your face — clearly visible, no guessing
  • Marco kissed — plush held close, lips on Marco, his face visible
  • Sticker in frame — sharp, readable, within 30° angle
  • Unlimited locations — every new spot earns EUR 5

What does NOT count

  • Sticker held in hand, on paper, or on loose objects (bottles, cups)
  • Face hidden, sunglasses, mask — we need to see you
  • No kiss, no Marco, sticker out of frame or too far
  • Digital edits, filters, or overlays
  • Same location twice
  • Inappropriate attire (swimwear at beach/pool is fine)

The 30-Sticker Challenge — Unlock Your Payout

30 stickers. 30 locations. One goal: unlock everything.

Think of this as a quest with a real reward at the end. Every sticker you place earns EUR 5 credit instantly. Every scan earns you referral commission. Your balance grows with every move you make — selfie credit and referral commission stacking together.

At 10 completed referral assessments, your entire accumulated balance unlocks for cash withdrawal via Stripe. That is not a distant milestone — people who place all 30 stickers in high-traffic spots reach it faster than you would expect.

Every sticker sitting in the pack is EUR 5 you are leaving on the table. Every day without placement is a referral someone else earns instead. At 9 referrals you are one away from unlocking your full payout — imagine stopping there.
