ACSO Consulting LTD ("we", "us", or "our"), registered in Bulgaria, operates the website www.lingograde.com and provides language learning services under the LingoGrade brand. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

We collect information you provide directly to us when you register for our services, enroll in a course, make a payment, or contact us. This may include your name, email address, phone number, and payment information.

When you make a purchase, payment processing is handled by Stripe, Inc. We do not store your full credit card details on our servers. Stripe's privacy policy is available at stripe.com/privacy.

We may also collect technical data automatically when you visit our website, including your IP address, browser type, device information, and pages visited, through server logs and cookies.

We use the information we collect to process enrollments and payments, deliver language learning services and course materials, communicate with you about your courses and progress, respond to your inquiries and provide support, send you relevant updates about our services, comply with legal obligations, and improve our website and services.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. 4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. All payment transactions are encrypted using SSL/TLS technology and processed through PCI-compliant payment infrastructure.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, including to satisfy legal, accounting, or reporting requirements. Course enrollment and transaction data are retained in accordance with applicable Bulgarian and EU law.

6. Your Rights Under GDPR

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data, restrict or object to our processing of your data, request data portability, and withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent.
